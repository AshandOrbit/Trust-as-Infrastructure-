A practical guide to sustainable living that saves you money while protecting the planet

About the Book

Everyday Offset is a 220-page comprehensive guide that proves sustainability doesn't require sacrifice—it creates savings. Written by the Offset Team, this book combines environmental science with practical financial wisdom to show how small changes in daily routines can dramatically reduce both your carbon footprint and household expenses.

From bathroom habits to energy efficiency, transportation to food choices, each section provides actionable strategies backed by research and real-world cost savings. No guilt, no perfection required—just honest, transparent guidance for making better choices that benefit both your wallet and the planet.

What You'll Learn

Bathroom & Personal Care: Reduce water usage, extend time between washes, switch to sustainable products, and save on heating and product costs.

Energy Efficiency: Smart home strategies, appliance optimization, and behavioral changes that cut utility bills.

Transportation: Cost-effective alternatives, maintenance tips, and planning strategies that reduce emissions.

Food & Consumption: Reduce waste, make informed choices, and save money on groceries while eating better.

And much more: Each section provides difficulty ratings, time commitments, and clear financial breakdowns so you can prioritize the changes that work for your life.
